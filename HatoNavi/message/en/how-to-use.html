Welcome to HatoNavi History!

This is a powerful viewing history management tool to upgrade your YouTube experience.
Let's master all its features!

📺

Convenient Viewing Support

Resume Playback: Close a video midway? No problem. It will automatically resume from where you left off.
Progress Bar: The red bar on the thumbnail shows how much you've watched.
Completion Mark: Videos watched over 95% are marked with a ↺ symbol.

🖱️

Quick Card Operations

Hover over a card to reveal buttons for quick organization.
Favorites (☆): Click the star icon to add or remove from favorites.
Tagging: Long-press the star icon to add custom tags.
Mark as Watched (✓): Manually mark an unwatched video as watched.
Delete from History (×): Remove unwanted history from the list (favorites are protected).

🔍

Instantly Find What You Want

Instantly narrow down your vast history by clicking the blue channel name or a tag in the tag bar. You can also perform advanced searches with the AND/OR switch.

💾

Secure Data Management

You can perform a backup (export) and restore (import) of your entire history from the button on the right side of the header. This offers peace of mind even when replacing your PC. On the 🏷️Tag Management screen, you can also rename, reorder, and delete tags in bulk.
