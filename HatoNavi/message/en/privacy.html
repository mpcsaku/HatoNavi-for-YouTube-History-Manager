Privacy Policy

We respect user privacy and are committed to ensuring a secure and comfortable experience with this extension.

Data is Stored Only on Your PC

All data, including viewing history, is stored only in a secure area on your own computer.

Your Viewing History is Private

Since all data is confined to your PC, it is physically impossible for any third party to access your viewing history.

We Do Not Access Account Information

We do not acquire or use any personal information linked to your Google/YouTube account, such as your name or email address.

Communication with Official YouTube Features to Display Video Information

To display thumbnails, video titles, and channel names on the history page, we utilize secure, officially provided features of YouTube.
Although the video ID is sent to YouTube's servers during this communication, this is a basic and secure part of the YouTube website's operation.

Data is Sent to an External Server Only if Video Information Cannot Be Obtained

If and only if video information cannot be obtained using the method above, the video ID will be sent to cloudfunctions.net, operated by Google, solely to retrieve the title and channel name.
This communication is also secure, and absolutely no data other than the video ID is transmitted.
