Privacy Policy

This extension is designed with respect for your privacy, so you can use it with confidence.

Your Data is Stored Only on Your PC

All data, including your watch history, is stored exclusively in a secure area on your computer.

We Never Transmit Your Data Externally

Your history data will never be sent over the internet to any destination without your permission.

No One Else Can See Your History

Since the data is confined to your PC, it is physically impossible for any third party to know your watch history.

We Do Not Access Your Account Information

We will never acquire or use personal information associated with your Google/YouTube account, such as your name or email address.

Communication is Only for Fetching Video Information

Only when video information (like titles) is missing, we securely communicate with official YouTube services to retrieve it. This process does not send any of your personal information.
